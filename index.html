Game rules

  • At the beginning of the game, the cards are automatically shuffled.
  • The course of the game is carried out clockwise.
  • If the wheel stops on an already opened card, the player can choose any other closed card.
  • The player must complete the task shown on the card.
  • When all the cards are revealed, the game ends and you can start a new game.
